1. Introduction

Edmonton Tamil Catholic Family (ETCF) ("we," "our," "us") is a non-profit religious organization operating in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. By engaging with ETCF, including attending our events, making donations, participating in activities, or using our website (www.edmontontamilcatholics.ca), you agree to comply with the following Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree with these terms, you should refrain from interacting with ETCF.

2. Membership & Participation

2.1 Eligibility

  • Membership is open to individuals and families who share the values and beliefs of the Catholic faith and wish to be part of the Tamil Catholic community in Edmonton.
  • Minors (under 18) require parental or guardian consent for participation.

2.2 Membership Rights & Responsibilities

  • Members are entitled to participate in religious services, community events, and decision-making processes as outlined in ETCF's internal policies.
  • Members must uphold the integrity of ETCF and adhere to its principles, constitution, and bylaws.
  • Any misconduct, disruptive behavior, or violation of ETCF policies may lead to suspension or termination of membership at the discretion of the governing body.

2.3 Revocation of Membership

  • ETCF reserves the right to revoke membership based on unethical behavior, violations of our constitution or bylaws, or any action that negatively impacts the organization or its members.
  • Appeals may be submitted in writing to the Executive Committee for review.

3. Events & Gatherings

  • ETCF organizes religious gatherings, cultural programs, and social events for the Tamil Catholic community.
  • Event participation is open to members and non-members, subject to specific event guidelines.
  • Attendees must adhere to venue regulations, codes of conduct, and respect religious practices during events.
  • ETCF is not responsible for any injuries, lost belongings, or incidents that may occur during events.

4. Donations & Financial Contributions

4.1 Donations

  • ETCF welcomes voluntary donations from members and well-wishers to support community initiatives, charity works, and operational expenses.
  • Donations can be made via cash, cheque, online payments, or other approved methods.
  • Donations are non-refundable except in cases of error or duplicate transactions.

4.2 Financial Transparency

  • ETCF maintains financial records in compliance with Canadian non-profit regulations.
  • Members may request access to financial statements and reports by submitting a formal request.

5. Privacy & Data Protection

5.1 Data Collection & Usage

  • Personal information collected by ETCF (such as name, email, phone number, event participation details, and donation records) is used strictly for organizational purposes.
  • ETCF does not sell or share personal data with third parties except for essential services (e.g., payment processing and email communications).
  • For more details, refer to our Privacy Policy: Privacy Policy.

5.2 Data Access & Modification

  • Members can request to review, update, or delete their personal information by contacting support@edmontontamilcatholics.ca.
  • To opt out of communications, email support@edmontontamilcatholics.ca with the subject "Unsubscribe me from ___".

6. Code of Conduct

  • Members and participants must show respect, inclusivity, and uphold Catholic values in all ETCF-related activities.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or any form of abusive behaviour will not be tolerated.
  • Violation of the code of conduct may result in suspension from activities or events.

7. Use of Media & Photography

  • ETCF reserves the right to use photographs, videos, and audio recordings taken during events for promotional, educational, or informational purposes.
  • Images will not include full names or personal details without written consent.
  • If you wish to opt out of media usage, you must inform the photographer, event organizers, or email connect@edmontontamilcatholics.ca with an identifying photo.
  • Participation in events implies consent unless a written opt-out request is provided.

8. Liability Disclaimer

  • ETCF is not responsible for any personal injury, loss, or damage incurred during events, meetings, or other activities.
  • Members and participants attend events at their own risk and must take personal precautions.
  • ETCF will not be liable for any third-party services or products associated with its activities.

9. Website & Digital Presence

9.1 Website Usage

  • Users must not misuse or tamper with ETCF’s website (www.edmontontamilcatholics.ca).
  • Content on the website is for informational purposes only and should not be copied or distributed without permission.
  • Unauthorized access or cyber-attacks against ETCF’s website will be reported to authorities.

9.2 Social Media Engagement

  • ETCF operates on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube to share community updates.
  • Members engaging with ETCF’s social media pages must follow platform policies and refrain from posting inappropriate content.
  • ETCF reserves the right to remove offensive comments or block users violating these guidelines.

10. Amendments & Updates

  • ETCF reserves the right to amend these Terms & Conditions at any time.
  • Updates will be communicated through the official WhatsApp group, website, and individual email notifications.
  • Continued participation in ETCF activities after updates implies acceptance of the revised Terms & Conditions.
