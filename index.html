About ETCF

We are Catholics joyfully celebrating the profound connection between our Catholic faith and the rich heritage of the Tamil language. Most of our members hail from India, Sri Lanka, and Singapore, all united by our shared Tamil-speaking background. Each month, we gather to celebrate the Holy Mass in Tamil, embracing our spiritual roots and cultural identity. Through the beauty of our Tamil prayers, hymns, and liturgy, we deepen our relationship with Jesus Christ and each other.

We are truly blessed to have the approval of the Catholic Archdiocese of Edmonton, allowing us to celebrate the Holy Mass in Tamil on the second Saturday of every month at St. Theresa's Catholic Church, Edmonton, AB.

Mission

Faith - Helping the community grow in Catholic spirituality through liturgical celebrations and feasts.
Tamil Culture - Celebrating and nurturing Tamil culture for the youth to pass on the heritage.
Community - Strengthening relationships among families and offering mutual support.
Charity - Serving the poor and needy through charitable work and outreach.
